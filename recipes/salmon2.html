Grilled Salmon 2

3 Ingredient Salsa-glazd Salmon

This is one of my favorite recipe. My mum used to cooked it every other Sunday for dinner.

Ingrediets

  • 5 oz Salmon
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 tbs chopped green onions

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 1000 F
  2. Mixed all the ingredients in a bowl
  3. Place fillet in a oven safe tray
  4. bake for 15 minutes
